Problem

Hanson Canada is a private education group providing secondary, post-secondary, and language programs to both domestic and international students. ... Hanson delivers Cambrian College programs exclusively to international students.

Hanson Canada is looking for a event system which icludes calender system, booking and payment system to incorporate, event info etc.

Registration system which alerts all the students to make registrations, upload documents such as work permits, pay for fees, monitor fees and give constant updates to the students.

Our third main objective would be to create a section which includes career services and alumni services to crete an area for career services, with a job/volunteer board, portal for employees to submit vacancies, platforms for students to directly apply to advertised vacancies through the web using uploaded resumes and cover letters.

Tools to allow students to upload video resumes, resume/cover letter checking function for grammer errors, career resources area, alumni section with alumni news and updates, platform/forum to link alumni mentors to current students and recent grades etc.
